System Requirements

Linux

  • Ubuntu 24.04+ or Debian Trixie+
  • Fedora 39+
  • Arch Linux (AUR)
  • glibc 2.38 or later
  • PipeWire or PulseAudio
  • Wayland or X11

macOS

  • macOS 12 Monterey or later
  • Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3) or Intel
  • 4GB RAM minimum
  • 500MB disk space
